Privacy policy

Your data, your rights

Amm Sa Appli is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We collect only what we need to deliver your app, and we never show ads or sell your information.

Information we collect

When you use the Application, we may collect your IP address, pages visited, time spent, operating system, approximate location, and build-related activity such as progress, test results, and change requests.

Google sign-in data

Registration uses Google OAuth 2.0. We access your display name, profile photo, and email address only. We do not access your password, contacts, Drive, or any other Google data.

Personal information

To deliver your app project, we may also store your name, email, address, phone number, and any ID or documentation you provide for verification purposes (Professional plan).

No advertising — ever

Amm Sa Appli contains zero advertisements. We do not use ad networks, we do not display third-party ads, and we will never sell your data to advertisers or marketing platforms.

Third-party access

Only anonymized, aggregated data is shared with external services (Google Play, Firebase). We may disclose data when required by law or to protect user safety. We never share for advertising.

Your rights & opt-out

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app. To delete your account data, contact us at jaayma.application@gmail.com. We respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

The Application is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a child under 13 has registered, we delete their data immediately.

Security

We implement physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Your project data is stored securely and accessible only to authorized team members.

This privacy policy is effective as of February 13, 2025. For questions, contact us at ammsaappli@gmail.com.
